SEDANS

A sedan or saloon is a passenger car in a three-box configuration with separate compartments for an engine, passengers, and cargo

SUVS

It refers to a type of car that sits high off the ground and which often has four-wheel drive and rugged styling.

LUXARY

A luxury car is a car that provides increased levels of comfort, equipment, amenities, quality, performance, and associated status compared to moderately priced cars.
